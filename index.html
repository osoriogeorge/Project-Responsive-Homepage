About me

Photographer specializing in human narratives and urban landscapes. Based in Barcelona but working globally, my work explores the intersection between identity and environment through a raw yet poetic aesthetic.

Trained at the Madrid School of Photography and with 8 years of experience in editorial projects and international exhibitions, I seek to capture moments that challenge the conventional perception of reality.

My clients include National Geographic, Vogue Spain, and independent art collectives.
